# Nextcloud Talk for Android TV - User Guide

## Getting Started

### Installation
1. Open the Google Play Store on your Android TV
2. Search for "Nextcloud Talk"
3. Select and install the app
4. The app will appear in your Android TV launcher

### First-Time Setup

#### Logging In with Your Remote

1. **Launch the App**
   - Find "Nextcloud Talk" in your Android TV launcher
   - Press OK on your remote to launch

2. **Enter Server URL**
   - The server input field will be highlighted automatically
   - Press OK to open the on-screen keyboard
   - Use arrow keys to navigate the keyboard
   - Type your server URL (e.g., `cloud.example.com`)
   - Select "Done" or press Back to close the keyboard
   - Navigate to the arrow button and press OK to continue

3. **Scan QR Code (Optional)**
   - Press Down arrow to focus on "Scan QR" button
   - Press OK to open QR scanner
   - Point your camera at the QR code
   - Login will complete automatically

4. **Browser Login**
   - A web page will open automatically
   - Use arrow keys to navigate between username and password fields
   - Press OK on each field to bring up the on-screen keyboard
   - Enter your username and password
   - Navigate to "Log in" button and press OK

5. **Grant Permissions**
   - When prompted, use arrow keys to navigate to "Allow"
   - Press OK to grant camera permission
   - Repeat for microphone permission

6. **You're Ready!**
   - The app will load your conversations
   - Use arrow keys to navigate
   - Press OK to select and start a call

## Tips for Best Experience

1. **Lighting**: Ensure good lighting in your room for better video quality
2. **Distance**: Sit at an appropriate distance from the TV (typically 6-10 feet)
3. **Network**: Use wired Ethernet connection for best call quality
4. **Audio**: Consider using external speakers for better sound quality
5. **Camera**: Position your TV camera at eye level if possible

## Troubleshooting

### App Not Appearing in TV Launcher
- Check if your device supports Android TV (not just Android on a TV screen)
- Ensure you downloaded the correct version from Play Store
- Try restarting your Android TV device

### Remote Not Working
- Check remote batteries
- Try re-pairing the remote with your TV
- Use the Android TV Remote app on your phone as backup

### Focus Issues
- Press the Back button and re-enter the screen
- Use the D-pad to manually navigate to visible buttons
- Restart the app if focus is lost

### Video Quality Issues
- Check your internet connection speed
- Move closer to your WiFi router or use Ethernet
- Close other apps using bandwidth
- Lower video quality in settings if available

### Microphone/Camera Not Working
- Check TV app permissions in Android TV Settings
- Ensure no other app is using the camera/microphone
- Restart the Android TV device
